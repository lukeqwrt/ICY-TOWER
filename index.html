SECURITY

At CoinToFish we care a lot about your safety, so we have designed a unique system to protect you. The website is connected to an external server that constantly monitors suspicious activity on your account and locks your account if necessary to protect it against theft.

We also offer you an extra factor of security by means of an unlock pattern when entering your account.

STAKING

We offer a fixed percentage staking system with which you can earn a full x% of what you enter and we will not take any of your earnings.

ANTIBOTS

We also include an anti bot system to make the gaming experience the best possible for you.

We have carefully calculated all the rewards offered to maximize your winnings and increase the durability of the game.

We also think about negative cases, so we have implemented a dynamic rewards system so that the durability of the game is as long as possible and the trade flows smoothly on the marketplace.

NO BNB FEES

You will not have to pay any fee to feed your fish or buy from our store, since everything is bought and done with the local currency

MARKET PLACE

Our team has worked hard to bring you the first real-time marketplace that currently exists in the world of NFTs. So, you will have to hurry if you want to buy an object before someone else.

PLAY & EARN

The game mode is very simple. You will only have to buy an egg, open it and with the fish that comes out start to earn money. Each fish can feed 2 times every 4 hours and will give you a reward for each feeding.

PVM & PVP

We plan to create the best fighting system you have ever seen in an NFT browser game. With a dynamic fighting system where you can interact and take actions that can decide the course of the battle.

DAILY REWARDS

Every 24 hours a button will be available in your account to claim your daily reward. Do not forget to claim it, maybe something unexpected will happen to you.

ORACLE SYSTEM

The price of all objects, eggs, canes, food ... will be automatically adjusted to the price of the token thanks to our oracle system.
